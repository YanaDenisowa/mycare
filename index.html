Accessible & affordable healthcare

For the first time, technology allows us to collect data outside of the actual treatment, to detect complications at an early stage, and digitally support adherence, which can lead to significantly better patient outcomes, improved care collaboration and lower treatment costs.

Myoncare starts precisely here to provide a disease agnostic care-coordination platform to providers, payers or manufacturers to create eco-systems around their treatment modalities or products.

Empowering automated
care coordination

Using myoncare's unique content management system, analog knowledge about a disease or product can be bundled into digital Care Pathways.

Content can be created as desired and linked in relation to a reference date. At the same time, it is possible to define which measures should be initiated if things do not go according to plan. Once created, pathways can be prescribed to any number of patients with the same condition.
